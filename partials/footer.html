Find Your Voice!

FYV! is a small project and brainchild of Tim Maddux, student at Nashville Software School. The idea is simple: our state legislators do actually read the emails that constituents send. This is a site that helps you find and save bills on issues close to your heart, and then attempts to nudge you in the direction of getting a simple email out to your state senator or representative. To get started, click here to down a current list of all state legislators with all their contact info. You can use this list to upload to your contacts list using pretty much every email program out there. Don't know how? Unlock the magic of Google, here.
