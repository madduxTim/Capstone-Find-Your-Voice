Find Your Voice!

FYV! is a small test project by Tim Maddux. The idea is simple: to encourage users to contact their state legislators with their thoughts about specific bills. The application helps locate bills by keyword search, and gives the user the ability to save a bill to their specific login. It then attempts to nudge them in the direction of getting a simple email out to your state senator or representative. To get started, click here to down a current list of all state legislators with all their contact info. You can use this list to upload to your contacts list using pretty much every email program out there. Don't know how? Unlock the magic of Google, here.
